Crop Rotation App

Select your vegetables by clicking the images below. Click again if you want to deselect the vegetable.

After you selected your vegetables click Create my garden button to set up your crop location plan. Don't worry if you change your mind - you will be able to re-select your vegetables later on.

Remove veg from the veggie bed by clicking on it. you can add more veg from the veg pool by selecting them and clicking Add more vegetables button.
